About Our Website

Helping users track and reduce food waste effectively.

Website Features

Our website includes the following key pages and functionalities:

Home Page

The main landing page where you can learn about the purpose of this project and get started with tracking food waste.

Data Entry

Log the amount of food wasted daily and hourly with ease. Simply enter the data, and our system will handle the rest.

Statistics

Visualize your data with interactive pie and bar charts to see waste trends over time, helping you take action to reduce food waste.

Our Mission

Our goal is to encourage mindful consumption by providing a tool to track and analyze food waste. By understanding waste patterns, we can make more informed decisions and contribute to reducing food waste.
